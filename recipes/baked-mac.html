Baked Macaroni and Cheese

Description

A rich and creamy baked mac and cheese with a golden, crispy cheese topping. This comfort food classic is perfect for family dinners, potlucks, or anytime you want something warm and satisfying.

Quick Facts

  • Prep Time: 10 mins
  • Cook Time: 35-40 mins
  • Total Time: 45-50 mins
  • Servings: About 6

Ingredients

  • 12 ounces elbow macaroni pasta
  • 2 cups whole milk
  • 1 large egg
  • 2 1/2 cups shredded cheddar cheese
  • 2 tablespoons butter (melted)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Set out a 2 quart baking dish, lightly grease.
  2. Boil macaroni until barely done, typically about 5 minutes. Drain and allow to cool a bit.
  3. Whisk egg and milk together in a large bowl, stir in butter and cheese.
  4. Place macaroni in the baking dish you prepared earlier. Pour mixture over the macaroni, season with salt and pepper and stir until combined. Press mixture evenly into a baking dish.
  5. Bake uncovered in the oven until the top is golden brown, typically about 30-40 minutes.
  6. Serve hot and try not to eat it all!
